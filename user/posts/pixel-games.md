### 为什么像素艺术？

像素艺术是一种在像素级别创建图像的数字艺术形式。它唤起对 8-bit 和 16-bit 时代游戏的怀旧感。

> "像素是数字艺术的原子。"

### Favorite Pixel Games

1. **Celeste** — A precision platformer with an incredible soundtrack
2. **Stardew Valley** — Farming, fishing, and friendship
3. **Undertale** — A game where you don't have to destroy anyone

### Why Pixel Art?

Pixel art is a form of digital art where images are created at the pixel level. It evokes nostalgia for 8-bit and 16-bit era gaming.

> "Pixels are the atoms of digital art."
