## 那座山

Celeste 是一个关于攀登一座山的游戏。但实际上是关于走出自己内心的黑暗。

## 像素艺术做到极致

Celeste 中的每个精灵都是手工制作的。有限的调色板迫使设计师通过姿态和时机来传达情感，而非面部细节。

## 游戏循环

- 跳跃、冲刺、攀墙
- 每个章节引入一个新机制
- 难度严苛但公平

## 原声音乐

Lena Raine 的配乐与游戏体验密不可分。芯片音乐元素完美地衬托了像素视觉风格。

## The Mountain

Celeste is a game about climbing a mountain. But it's really about climbing out of your own darkness.

## Pixel Art Done Right

Every sprite in Celeste is hand-crafted. The limited palette forces designers to convey emotion through posture and timing rather than facial detail.

## Gameplay Loop

- Jump, dash, wall-climb
- Each chapter introduces a new mechanic
- Difficulty is punishing but fair

## The Soundtrack

Lena Raine's score is inseparable from the experience. The chiptune elements complement the pixel visuals perfectly.
